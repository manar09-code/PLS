Supporting Children in Palestine, Lebanon, and Syria During Times of Crisis

Our Mission

The PLS Rescue Organization is committed to providing critical support to children and families in Palestine, Lebanon, and Syria. Through emergency relief, food distribution, clothing, and shelter, we strive to bring hope and stability to those affected by war, poverty, and natural disasters. Together, we can ensure a brighter future for the most vulnerable.

Helping Palestinians Enjoy the Eid

Since the Nakbah, countless Palestinian families have been displaced, leaving children without homes, education, or proper healthcare. The PLS Rescue Organization focuses on rebuilding hope by providing access to essential supplies, schooling, and mental health support for children enduring the hardships of occupation and displacement.

Your contribution can help restore dignity and security to these children, offering them a chance to dream and thrive despite the challenges.

Helping with Rebuilding Destroyed Houses in Lebanon

Lebanon faces ongoing economic and political crises, leaving many families struggling to survive. Children are among the most affected, especially during harsh winters when food, clothing, and shelter are in short supply. PLS Rescue steps in to provide warm clothing, nourishing meals, and heated shelters to ensure their safety and comfort.

Stand with us to protect the most vulnerable and give them a fighting chance during these difficult times.

Helping Children in Syria

Years of war in Syria have left millions of children without homes, healthcare, or education. Many face freezing winters with no protection against the cold. PLS Rescue delivers essential aid such as blankets, warm clothes, and food packs to displaced families living in refugee camps.

Together, we can bring warmth, care, and hope to children in Syria who deserve a chance to rebuild their lives.
